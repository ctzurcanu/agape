Astra polorum
Cuncta chorique,
Solque sororque, 
Lumina caeli.

Omnipotentem
Semper adorent
Et benedicant
Omne per aevum

Omne per aevum

English:

The stars of the poles
All their choirs,
And the sun and sister,
The lights of heaven.

May the Almighty
Always adore
And bless
All through the ages

So also the lymph
And the heavenly ones,
Dew and rain,
Every spirit.

Fire and heat,
Frost and frost,
Cold and hot
And frost.

Snow and ice,
Night and day,
Light and darkness,
Lightning, clouds.

Dry land, mountains,
Growths, clusters,
Rivers, fountains,
Ponts and waves.

All living things,
Which the sea carries,
Which the air carries,
And the earth nourishes.

The whole race of men,
Israel itself
And Christ's servants,
Every one of them.

Saints and humble, 
with kind hearts, 
Three little ones, 
overcoming the flames of the furnace, 
Ready to obey the tyrant's commands.
